Enhypen

ENHYPEN is a South Korean K-pop boy group formed through the survival show I-LAND, which was a joint project between HYBE Corporation (formerly BigHit Entertainment, BTS's company) and CJ ENM. The group debuted on November 30, 2020, under the label BELIFT LAB, a joint venture between HYBE and CJ ENM.
